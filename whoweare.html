Who are we ?

For the moment, the collective is only made up of myself, a 2nd year student at Sciences Po Bordeaux.

I created this site to share a project that is very close to my heart, that of allowing endangered languages to leave a legacy available free of charge to anyone. This initiative is part of my final Writing and Rhetoric project.

The site is still in the early stages, but it is intended to continue to evolve and be available online for as long as possible. It will never include a lucrative dimension or ads, but any volunteer help is welcome. To learn more about this project, you can click here.
